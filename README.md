## 🎯 Problem Statement

Farmers today sell through middlemen and receive far below market price. There is no real-time price visibility, no direct buyer connection, and no logistics support. **Agri Market Connect** eliminates all of this.

---

## ✨ Features

### 👨‍🌾 Farmer Hub
- ✅ Crop listing with organic certification upload
- ✅ **AI Crop Disease Detector** (TensorFlow Lite simulation) — laser scan animation + diagnosis
- ✅ **AI Price Trend Predictor** — interactive SVG charts (tomorrow / weekly / monthly)
- ✅ IoT Soil Moisture monitoring with smart irrigation suggestions
- ✅ Incoming orders table with logistics booking

### 🛒 Buyer Portal
- ✅ Live GPS Canvas Map — animated nearby farm markers + truck tracking
- ✅ **Live E-Negotiation Chat** — AI-powered real-time price bargaining
- ✅ Shopping cart with checkout
- ✅ Order delivery tracker (step-by-step pipeline)

### 📊 Admin Panel
- ✅ Bar chart — Daily crop sales volume
- ✅ Donut pie chart — Category distribution
- ✅ Area chart — Revenue growth trend
- ✅ Farmer / Buyer verification center
- ✅ Complaint resolution desk

### 🔗 Advanced Features
- ✅ **Blockchain Traceability** — Full farm-to-table ledger with cryptographic hashes + QR code
- ✅ **Multi-lingual AI Chatbot** — English | Tamil (தமிழ்) | Hindi (हिंदी)
- ✅ **Voice Search** — Speak crop names in English or Tamil
- ✅ **Smart Logistics** — Truck class selector, route optimization, carbon footprint calculator
- ✅ **Secure Payment Modal** — UPI QR simulation + Wallet escrow
- ✅ **Toast Notification System** — Real-time platform alerts
